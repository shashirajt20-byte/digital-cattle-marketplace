## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders of the CivoraX Internship Program pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the CivoraX team at [contact@civoranexus.com](mailto:contact@civoranexus.com).

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
